ush.pw is a tool for shortening URL that turns long and messy URLs into short ones, ideal for social media and chat. Simply paste the long URL and click the button. You can even create so called "branded" links (link with your own custom phrase) provided the phrase is not already used for some other link. On top of that, you can also check how many clicks your short link recieved in real time. Finally, there's also a simpe JSON API, if you like to dabble in coding stuff. And it's all for free, no ads or any similar nonsense. Enjoy!
